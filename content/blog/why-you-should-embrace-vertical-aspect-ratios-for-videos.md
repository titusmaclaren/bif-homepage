Just a quick one today - but important nonetheless.

![](/blog/why-you-should-embrace-vertical-aspect-ratios-for-videos-hero.jpg)

Because the viewer doesn't have to click through and rotate their phone to utilize the full screen, vertical aspect ratios have much higher engagement.

To do this with a proper camera, there are 2 main methods:

- Use a photography tripod and film in portrait orientation (camera on it's side), then create a 9:16 video sequence and rotate the footage to fit.
- Use a video tripod, allowing for smooth pans and tilts, then crop the sequence in post. Then move each clip left and right to get the right framing. This method allows for a regular 16:9 aspect ratio version and a square or vertical version.

For a resolution that will work well across social media we recommend cropping to 864x1080.
